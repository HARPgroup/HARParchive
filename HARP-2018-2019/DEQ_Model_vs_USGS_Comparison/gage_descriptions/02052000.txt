This river segment follows part of the flow of the Meherrin River. The gage is located in Emporia City, VA (Lat 36°41'24", Long 77°32'27") approximately 0.3 miles north of Emporia, VA. Drainage area is 744 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1951 and is still taking data. The flow in this area is regulated by the Virginia Electric Power Company’s dam that is 0.8 miles upstream.
