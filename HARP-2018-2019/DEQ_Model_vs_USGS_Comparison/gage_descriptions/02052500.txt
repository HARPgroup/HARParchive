This river segment follows part of the flow of the Fountains Creek, a tributary of the Meherrin River. The gage is located in Greensville County, VA (Lat 36°36'55", Long 77°42'00") approximately 10 miles southeast of Emporia, VA. Drainage area is 68.7 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1953 and was decommissioned in 1997. For this reason the analysis was run from 1984-10-01 to 1996-09-30. There are no known anthropogenic alterations that would affect the flow of this area.
