This river segment follows part of the flow of the Blackwater River, a tributary of the Roanoke River. The gage is located in Franklin County, VA (Lat 37°02'42", Long 79°50'40") approximately 20 miles southeast of Salem, VA. Drainage area is 115 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1976 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
