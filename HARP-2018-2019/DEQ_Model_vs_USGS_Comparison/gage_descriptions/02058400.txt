This river segment follows part of the flow of the Pigg River, a tributary of the Roanoke River. The gage is located in Pittsylvania County, VA (Lat 36°56'45", Long 79°31'30") approximately 26 miles northeast of Martinsville, VA. Drainage area is 351 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1963 and is still taking data. There is a small amount of diurnal fluctuation that has been recorded but the source is unknown.
