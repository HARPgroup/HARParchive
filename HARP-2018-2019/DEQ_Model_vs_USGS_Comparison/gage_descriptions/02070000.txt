This river segment follows part of the flow of the North Mayo River, a tributary of the Dan River. The gage is located in Henry County, VA (Lat 36°34'05", Long 79°59'15") approximately 10 miles southwest of Martinsville, VA. Drainage area is 108 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1928 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
