This river segment follows part of the flow of the New River. The gage is located in Pulaski County, VA (Lat 36°56'15", Long 80°44'45") approximately 21 miles north of Galax, VA. Drainage area is 2212 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1929 and is still taking data. Flow in this area is regulated by the American Electric Power Company’s power plants at Buck and Byllesby approximately 25 miles upstream. This station is also 21 miles upstream of Claytor Dam, which is also owned and operated by the American Electric Power Company.
