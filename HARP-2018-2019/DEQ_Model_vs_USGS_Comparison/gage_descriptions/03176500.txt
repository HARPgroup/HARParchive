This river segment follows part of the flow of the New River. The gage is located in Giles County, VA (Lat 37°22'22", Long 80°51'39") approximately 23 miles northwest of Radford, VA. Drainage area is 3783 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1927 and is still taking data. The Claytor dam and American Electric Power Company Power Plant is 55 miles upstream which causes a diversion of water. The water is withdrawn upstream and discharged into the East River just above its confluence with the New River.
