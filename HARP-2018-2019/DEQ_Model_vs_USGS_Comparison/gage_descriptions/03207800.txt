This river segment follows part of the flow of the Levisa Fork of the Big Sandy River. The gage is located in Buchanan County, VA (Lat 37°21'13", Long 82°11'45") approximately 40 miles northeast of Norton, VA. Drainage area is 297 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1967 and is still taking data. This area is not regulated and should not have any anthropogenic alterations to the flow.
