This river segment follows part of the flow of the North Fork of the Holston River, a tributary of the Tennessee River. The gage is located in Smyth County, VA (Lat 36°53'48", Long 81°44'47") approximately 32 miles northeast of Bristol, VA. Drainage area is 221 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1907 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
