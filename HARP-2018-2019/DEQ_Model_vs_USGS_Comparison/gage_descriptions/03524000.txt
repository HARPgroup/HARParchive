This river segment follows part of the flow of the Clinch River, a tributary of the Tennessee River. The gage is located in Russell County, VA (Lat 36°56'41", Long 82°09'18") approximately 24 miles north of Bristol, VA. Drainage area is 533 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1920 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
