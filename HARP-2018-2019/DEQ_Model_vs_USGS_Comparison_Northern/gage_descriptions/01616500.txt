This river segment follows part of the flow of the Opequon Creek, a tributary of the Potomac. The gage is located in Berkeley County (Lat. 39°25'25.4", Long. -77°56'19""), approximately 19 miles north of Berryville, VA. Drainage area is 273 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1947 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
