This river segment follows part of the flow of the South River, a tributary of the Potomac. The gage is located in Waynesboro County (Lat. 38°05'19.5", Long. -78°52'37.1"), approximately 2 miles northeast of Waynesboro, VA. Drainage area is 148 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1974 but was decommissioned in 2018. There are discharges from wastewater treatment plants upstream, originating from well fields.
