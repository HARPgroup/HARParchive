This river segment follows part of the flow of the Shenandoah River, a tributary of the Potomac. The gage is located in Jefferson County (Lat. 39°16'55.4", Long. -77°47'21""), approximately 8.1 miles west of Lovettsville, VA. Drainage area is 3041 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1895 and is still taking data. There is some regulation by upstream hydroelectric plants, including that of the Potomac Light and Power Company, 0.5 mi upstream.
