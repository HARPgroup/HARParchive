This river segment follows part of the flow of the Goose Creek, a tributary of the Potomac. The gage is located in Loudoun County (Lat. 39°01'10.4", Long. -77°34'39.0"), approximately 6.1 miles south of Leesburg, VA. Drainage area is 332 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1909 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
