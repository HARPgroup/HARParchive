This river segment follows part of the flow of the South Fork of Quantico Creek, a tributary of the Potomac. The gage is located in Prince William County (Lat. 38°35'14.4", Long. -77°25'42.9"), approximately 6 miles west of Dumfries, VA. Drainage area is 7.62 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1951 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
