This river segment follows part of the flow of the Rapidan River, a tributary of the Rappahannock. The gage is located in Madison County (Lat. 38°16'50.5", Long. -78°20'25.0"), approximately 5.2 miles east of Standardsville, VA. Drainage area is 115 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1942 and is still taking data. There has been a diversion 0.4 mi upstream since 1973.
