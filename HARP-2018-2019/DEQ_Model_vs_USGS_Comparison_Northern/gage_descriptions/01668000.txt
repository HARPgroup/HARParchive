This river segment follows part of the flow of the Rapphannock River, a tributary of the Rappahannock. The gage is located in Stafford County (Lat. 38°18'30.5", Long. -77°31'44.9"), approximately 2.3 miles northwest of Fredericksburg, VA. Drainage area is 1595 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1907 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
