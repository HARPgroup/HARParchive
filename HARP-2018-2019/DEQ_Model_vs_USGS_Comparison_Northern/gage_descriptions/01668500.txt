This river segment follows part of the flow of the Cat Point Creek, a tributary of the Rappahannock. The gage is located in Richmond County (Lat. 38°02'23.5", Long. -76°49'36.9"), approximately 6.7 miles northwest of Warsaw, VA. Drainage area is 45.6 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1943 but was decommissioned in 1999. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
