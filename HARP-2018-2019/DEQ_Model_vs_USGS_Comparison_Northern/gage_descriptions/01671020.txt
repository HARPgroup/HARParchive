This river segment follows part of the flow of the North Anna River, a tributary of the York. The gage is located in Caroline County (Lat. 37°51'00.5", Long. -77°25'39.9"), approximately 7 miles northeast of Ashland, VA. Drainage area is 462 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1979 and is still taking data. Since 1973 there has been a diversion at a point 0.8 mi upstream.
