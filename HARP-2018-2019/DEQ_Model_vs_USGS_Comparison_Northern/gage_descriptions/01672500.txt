This river segment follows part of the flow of the South Anna River, a tributary of the York. The gage is located in Hanover County (Lat. 37°47'48.5", Long. -77°32'55.9"), approximately 5.1 miles northwest of Ashland, VA. Drainage area is 395 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1930 and is still taking data. Since 1966, a diversion 150 ft upstream has averaged less than 0.6 cfs, but the capacity of the diversion is about 1.5 cfs.
