This river segment follows part of the flow of the Mattaponi River, a tributary of the York. The gage is located in King and Queen County (Lat. 37°53'02.0", Long. -77°09'55.0"), approximately 15 miles southeast of Bowling Green, VA. Drainage area is 603 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1941 and is still taking data. Diurnal fluctuations at times during low flow are caused by a gristmill on the Po River.
