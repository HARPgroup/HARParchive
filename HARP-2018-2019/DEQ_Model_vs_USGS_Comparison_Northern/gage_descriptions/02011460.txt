This river segment follows part of the flow of the Back Creek, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Bath County (Lat. 38°14'43.4", Long. -79°46'07.2"), approximately 15 miles southwest of Monterey, VA. Drainage area is 60.9 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1974 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
