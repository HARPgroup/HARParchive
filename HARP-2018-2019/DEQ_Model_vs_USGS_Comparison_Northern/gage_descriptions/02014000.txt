This river segment follows part of the flow of the Potts Creek, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Alleghany County (Lat. 37°43'44.5", Long. -80°02'32.2"), approximately 4.6 miles southwest of Covington, VA. Drainage area is 153 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1928 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
