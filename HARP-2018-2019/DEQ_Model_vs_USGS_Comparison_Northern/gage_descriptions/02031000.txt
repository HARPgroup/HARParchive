This river segment follows part of the flow of the Mechums River, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Albemarle County (Lat. 38°06'09.5", Long. -78°35'34.1"), approximately 7.7 miles northwest of Charlottesville, VA. Drainage area is 95.3 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1942 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
