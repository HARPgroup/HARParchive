This river segment follows part of the flow of the North Fork of Rivanna, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Albemarle County (Lat. 38°05'16.5", Long. -78°24'43.0"), approximately 5.3 miles northeast of Charlottesville, VA. Drainage area is 174 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1970 but was decommissioned in 1992. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
