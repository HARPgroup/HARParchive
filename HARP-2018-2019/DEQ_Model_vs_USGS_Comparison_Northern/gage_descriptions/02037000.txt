This river segment follows part of the flow of the James, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Henrico County (Lat. 37°33'52.5", Long. -77°34'27.0"), approximately 6 miles northwest  of Richmond, VA. Drainage area is 66.6 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1936 and is still taking data. A canal diverts water from the James River 1,200 ft upstream at Bosher Dam and discharges into the river at several points downstream from this gaging station, near Richmond.
