This river segment follows part of the flow of the Meherrin River. The gage is located in Brunswick County, VA (Lat 36°43'0", Long 77°49'55") approximately 16 miles west of Emporia, VA. Drainage area is 552 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1929 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
