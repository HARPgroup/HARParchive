This river segment follows part of the flow of the Goose Creek, a tributary of the Roanoke River. The gage is located in Bedford County, VA (Lat 37°10'23", Long 79°31'14") approximately 27 miles southwest of Lynchburg, VA. Drainage area is 188 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1930 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions. Prior to 1954 there was a mill upstream but it has since been decommissioned.
