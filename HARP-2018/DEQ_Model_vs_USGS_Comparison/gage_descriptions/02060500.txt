This river segment follows part of the flow of the Roanoke River. The gage is located in Pittsylvania County, VA (Lat 37°06'16", Long 79°17'44") approximately 23 miles south of Lynchburg, VA. Drainage area is 1782 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1930 and is still taking data. This area is regulated by the Smith Mount and Leesville power plants.
