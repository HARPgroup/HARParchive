This river segment follows part of the flow of the South Mayo River, a tributary of the Dan River. The gage is located in Patrick County, VA (Lat 36°34'15", Long 80°07'47") approximately 17 miles southwest of Martinsville, VA. Drainage area is 85.5 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1962 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
