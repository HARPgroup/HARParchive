This river segment follows part of the flow of the Dan River, a tributary of the Roanoke River. The gage is located in Rockingham County, NC (Lat 36°24'45", Long 79°49'34") approximately 27 miles southwest of Danville, VA. Drainage area is 1053 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1939 and is still taking data. There are slight diurnal fluctuations and regulations at low flow stages caused by the Talbott and Townes reservoirs.
