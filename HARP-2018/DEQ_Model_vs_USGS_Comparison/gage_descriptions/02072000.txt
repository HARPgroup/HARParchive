This river segment follows part of the flow of the Smith River, a tributary of the Dan River. The gage is located in Franklin County, VA (Lat 36°46'50", Long 80°01'30") approximately 10 miles northwest of Martinsville, VA. Drainage area is 215 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1946 and is still taking data. The Philpott Dam and Reservoir is located 900 ft upstream.
