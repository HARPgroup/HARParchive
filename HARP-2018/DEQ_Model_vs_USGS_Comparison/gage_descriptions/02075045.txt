This river segment follows part of the flow of the Dan River. The gage is located in Pittsylvania County, VA (Lat 36°33'45", Long 79°22'12") approximately 2 miles southeast of Danville, VA. Drainage area is 2116 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1995 and is still taking data. There are a number of dams and mills located in Danville that are expected to regulate the flow of this area, especially during low flow conditions.
