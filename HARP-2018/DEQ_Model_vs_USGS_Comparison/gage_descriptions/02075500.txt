This river segment follows part of the flow of the Dan River. The gage is located in Halifax County, VA (Lat 36°38'32", Long 79°05'23") approximately 17 miles northeast of Danville, VA. Drainage area is 2587 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1950 and is still taking data. There are a number of dams and mills in Danville that regulated the low-flow conditions in this area.
