This river segment follows part of the flow of the Banister River, a tributary of the Dan River. The gage is located in Halifax County, VA (Lat 36°46'35", Long 78°54'58") approximately 30 miles northeast of Danville, VA. Drainage area is 547 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1904 and is still taking data. Flow in this area is regulated by a reservoir and hydroelectric generating facility about a half mile upstream.
