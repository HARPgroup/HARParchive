This river segment follows part of the flow of the Hyco River, a tributary of the Dan River. The gage is located in Person County, NC (Lat 36°31'21", Long 78°58'51") approximately 24 miles southeast of Danville, VA. Drainage area is 202 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1973 and is still taking data. It is regulated by the Afterbay Dam which is 200ft upstream of the gage. The cities of Roxboro and Oxford use the Dam in cases of emergency to supply their citizens with water.
