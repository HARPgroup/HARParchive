This river segment follows part of the flow of the South Fork of the New River. The gage is located in Ashe County, NC (Lat 36°23'36", Long 81°24'25") approximately 33 miles southwest of Galax, VA. Drainage area is 205 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1924 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
