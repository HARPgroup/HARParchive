This river segment follows part of the flow of the Walker Creek, a tributary of the New River. The gage is located in Giles County, VA (Lat 37°16'05", Long 80°42'35") approximately 12 miles northwest of Radford, VA. Drainage area is 299 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1938 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
