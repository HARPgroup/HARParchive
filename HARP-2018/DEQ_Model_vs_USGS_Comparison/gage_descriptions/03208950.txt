This river segment follows part of the flow of the Cranes Nest River, a tributary of the Big Sandy River. The gage is located in Dickenson County, VA (Lat 37°07'26", Long 82°26'20") approximately 17 miles northeast of Norton, VA. Drainage area is 66.5 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1963 and is still taking data. This area is not regulated and should not have any man-made alterations that could affect flow conditions.
