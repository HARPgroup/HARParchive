This river segment follows part of the flow of the South Fork of the Holston River, a tributary of the Tennessee River. The gage is located in Smyth County, VA (Lat 36°45'37", Long 81°37'53") approximately 33 miles northeast of Bristol, VA. Drainage area is 76.6 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1920 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
