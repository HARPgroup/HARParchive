This river segment follows part of the flow of the Middle Fork of the Houston River, a tributary of the Tennessee River. The gage is located in Washington County, VA (Lat 36°42'47", Long 81°49'08") approximately 22 miles northeast of Bristol, VA. Drainage area is 206 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1931 and is still taking data. The Edmondson Power Company Dam was located 0.9 miles upstream of this gage but was decommissioned and removed in 1982.
