This river segment follows part of the flow of the Powell River, a tributary of the Tennessee  River. The gage is located in Lee County, VA (Lat 36°39'43", Long 83°05'42") approximately 32 miles southwest of Norton, VA. Drainage area is 319 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1931 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
