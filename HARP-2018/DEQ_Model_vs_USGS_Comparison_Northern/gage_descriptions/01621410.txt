This river segment follows part of the flow of the Blacks Run, a tributary of the Potomac. The gage is located in Rockingham County (Lat. 38°25'19.5", Long. -78°53'15.0"), approximately 1.2 miles southwest of Harrisonburg, VA. Drainage area is 10.9 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 2000 but was decommissioned in 2002. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
