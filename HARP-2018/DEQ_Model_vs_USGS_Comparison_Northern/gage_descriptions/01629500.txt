This river segment follows part of the flow of the South Fork of Shenandoah, a tributary of the Potomac. The gage is located in Page County (Lat. 38°38'46.4", Long. -78°32'05.0"), approximately 4.5 miles west of Luray, VA. Drainage area is 1372 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1925 and is still taking data. Diurnal fluctuations at low and medium flow are caused by powerplant 10 mi upstream.
