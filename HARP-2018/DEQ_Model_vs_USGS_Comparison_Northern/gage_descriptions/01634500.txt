This river segment follows part of the flow of the Cedar Creek, a tributary of the Potomac. The gage is located in Frederick County (Lat. 39°04'52.4", Long. -78°19'47.0"), approximately 5.8 miles west  of Stephens City, VA. Drainage area is 102 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1937 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
