This river segment follows part of the flow of the Accotink Creek, a tributary of the Potomac. The gage is located in Fairfax County (Lat. 38°48'46.4", Long. -77°13'41.9"), approximately 7.6 miles west of Alexandria, VA. Drainage area is 23.9 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1947 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
