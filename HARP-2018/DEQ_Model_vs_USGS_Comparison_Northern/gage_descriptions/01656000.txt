This river segment follows part of the flow of the Cedar Run, a tributary of the Potomac. The gage is located in Fauquier County (Lat. 38°38'12.4", Long. -77°37'31.0"), approximately 11 miles southwest of Manassas, VA. Drainage area is 93.4 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1950 and is still taking data. There are some diurnal fluctuation at low flow from an unknown cause.
