This river segment follows part of the flow of the Aquia Creek, a tributary of the Potomac. The gage is located in Stafford County (Lat. 38°29'25.4", Long. -77°26'00.9"), approximately 8 miles southwest of Dumfries, VA. Drainage area is 35 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1971 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
