This river segment follows part of the flow of the Robinson River, a tributary of the Rappahannock. The gage is located in Madison County (Lat. 38°19'30.5", Long. -78°05'45.0"), approximately 5.4 miles north of Orange, VA. Drainage area is 179 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1943 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
