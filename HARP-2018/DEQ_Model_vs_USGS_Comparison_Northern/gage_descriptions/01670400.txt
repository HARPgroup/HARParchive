This river segment follows part of the flow of the Mattaponi River, a tributary of the York. The gage is located in Spotsylvania County (Lat. 38°00'46.5", Long. -77°42'05.0"), approximately 11 miles east  of Mineral, VA. Drainage area is 342 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1978 and is still taking data. Flow here is regulated by Lake Anna which is 0.5 mi upstream.
