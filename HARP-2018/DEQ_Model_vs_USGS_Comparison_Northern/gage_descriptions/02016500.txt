This river segment follows part of the flow of the James River, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Botetourt County (Lat. 37°46'25.5", Long. -79°47'04.2"), approximately 1.8 miles southeast of Iron Gate, VA. Drainage area is 1371 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1925 and is still taking data. Flow has been regulated since December 1979 by Lake Moomaw (station 02011795) 43.7 mi upstream; since October 1984 by Back Creek Lake 71.7 mi upstream; and since January 1985 by Little Back Creek Lake 74.8 mi upstream, amounts unknown.
