This river segment follows part of the flow of the Catawba Creek, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Botetourt County (Lat. 37°28'05.5", Long. -80°00'19.2"), approximately 7.7 miles west of Fincastle, VA. Drainage area is 34.3 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1943 and is still taking data. From October 1953 to October 1976, monthly means were adjusted for withdrawals by the Citadel Cement Corporation.
