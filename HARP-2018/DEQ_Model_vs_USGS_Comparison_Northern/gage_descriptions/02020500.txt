This river segment follows part of the flow of the Calfpasture River, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Rockbridge County (Lat. 37°59'16.5", Long. -79°29'37.2"), approximately 1 mile east of Goshen, VA. Drainage area is 141 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1938 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
