This river segment follows part of the flow of the Maury River, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Rockbridge County (Lat. 37°54'26.5", Long. -79°25'19.1"), approximately 8.7 miles north of Lexington, VA. Drainage area is 329 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1928 and is still taking data. Since 1966, there have been some regulation at times by Lake Merriweather on Little Calfpasture River.
