This river segment follows part of the flow of the Maury River, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Rockbridge County (Lat. 37°45'45.5", Long. -79°23'29.1"), approximately 2.8 miles northwest of Buena Vista, VA. Drainage area is 647 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1938 and is still taking data. Since 1966, there has been some regulation at times by Lake Merriweather on Little Calfpasture River.
