This river segment follows part of the flow of the James River, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Nelson County (Lat. 37°32'10.5", Long. -78°49'46.1"), approximately 12 miles north of Appomattox, VA. Drainage area is 3649 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1925 and is still taking data. Large diurnal fluctuations are caused by powerplants upstream. Flow has been regulated since December 1979 by Lake Moomaw (station 02011795) 158.3 mi upstream; since October 1984 by Back Creek Lake 186.3 mi upstream; and since January 1985 by Little Back Creek Lake 189.4 mi upstream, amount unknown.
