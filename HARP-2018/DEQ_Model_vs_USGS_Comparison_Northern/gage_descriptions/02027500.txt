This river segment follows part of the flow of the Piney River, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Amherst County (Lat. 37°42'08.5", Long. -79°01'39.1"), approximately 8.5 miles north of Amherst, VA. Drainage area is 47.7 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1949 and is still taking data. Periodic dewatering of upstream quarries adds a small amount of inflow at times.
