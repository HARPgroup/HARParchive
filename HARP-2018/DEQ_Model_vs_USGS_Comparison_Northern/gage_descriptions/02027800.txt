This river segment follows part of the flow of the Buffalo River, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Nelson County (Lat. 37°36'20.5", Long. -78°55'24.1"), approximately 7.2 miles east of Amherst, VA. Drainage area is 148 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1960 but was decommissioned in 1995. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
