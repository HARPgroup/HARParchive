This river segment follows part of the flow of the Hardware River, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in Fluvanna County (Lat. 37°48'45.5", Long. -78°27'20.0"), approximately 2.1 miles east of Scottsville, VA. Drainage area is 116 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1938 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
