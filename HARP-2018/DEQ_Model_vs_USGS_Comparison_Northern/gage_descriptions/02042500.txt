This river segment follows part of the flow of the Chickahominy River, a tributary of the James. The gage is located in New Kent County (Lat. 37°26'10.5", Long. -77°03'38.9"), approximately 15.3 miles northwest of Claremont, VA. Drainage area is 251 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1942 and is still taking data. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
