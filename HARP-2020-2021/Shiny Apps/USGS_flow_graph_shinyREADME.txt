USGS_flow_graph_shiny.R

The USGS_flow_graph_shiny script includes the fluidpage, server, and runApp portion of the shiny code. 
The app shows a hydrograph of the stream flow or a given USGS gage over a given time period.

The variables that can be changed on the app to change the heatmap are:

-Gage ID: The desired USGS gage ID for the hydrograph
-Start Date: The desired start date for the hydrograph
-End Date: The desired end date for the hydrograph
